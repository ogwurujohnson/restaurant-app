Get to Know the Team

Restaurant Passport is designed to be your one-stop destination for anything you can think of that is about or related to restaurants.

At Restaurant Passport, we believe everyone deserves to have the power to choose where they go to eat and have their "me time". Innovation and simplicity makes us happy: our goal is to remove barriers of locating restaurants and keep track of visited ones, which can prevent individuals from visiting and trying out new spots. We know that the Internet is an overwhelming space and finding just the information you need can still be a frustrating and time-consuming process. We're excited to help!
